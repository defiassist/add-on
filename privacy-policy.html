Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026.

Introduction

DeFi Assist is a Google Sheets™ add-on for DeFi portfolio tracking. This Privacy Policy explains what data we process, how we use it, and what control you have.

Data We Process

To provide the add-on functionality, we process:

  • Wallet addresses provided by the user.
  • Add-on configuration data such as update frequency, update time, timezone, selected chains, and trigger identifiers.
  • Portfolio and transaction data returned by supported DeFi data providers (for example protocol/token names, chain, timestamps, token amounts, and value metrics).
  • Master Dashboard inputs when used (for example wallet labels and linked spreadsheet URLs/IDs).
  • Technical logs and cache entries required for reliability and troubleshooting.

Where Data Is Stored

Data is stored primarily in your Google Sheets™ document and in Google Apps Script™ document/user properties and cache associated with that document.

We do not maintain a separate proprietary backend database for your wallet portfolio records.

How We Use Data

We use processed data to:

  • fetch and refresh DeFi portfolio and transaction data,
  • calculate and display metrics in your spreadsheet,
  • run scheduled update jobs requested by the user,
  • monitor service reliability and fix errors.

Third-Party Services and Data Transfer

To operate the core features, wallet addresses and request parameters are sent to third-party DeFi APIs (currently DeBank endpoints configured in the add-on).

We do not sell or rent your data. We share data only as required to provide the service functionality.

Google API Services User Data Policy

DeFi Assist's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

For details, please visit the Google API Services User Data Policy.

Managing Access to Your Google Account™

You can manage app permissions at any time from your Google Account™ settings. For detailed instructions on how Google helps users share data safely, see this Google support article.

Data Retention and User Control

You can remove data by deleting spreadsheet rows/sheets and revoking add-on access in your Google Account™ settings.

Cached and technical data may be retained temporarily for performance and stability purposes.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards available within Google Apps Script™ and Google Sheets™ environments. However, no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

For privacy questions, contact us at defiassist@gmail.com.
