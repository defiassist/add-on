Support and FAQ

Last updated: February 17, 2026.

How does DeFi Assist work?

1. Setup: In Google Sheets™, open the add-on sidebar, enter a wallet address, choose update frequency and timezone, then submit.

2. Portfolio sync: DeFi Assist fetches protocol and token data and writes it to your spreadsheet.

3. Metrics: You can run transaction processing and calculate key metrics (IRR, ROI, P&L, investments, and withdrawals).

What modes are available?

Wallet Tracker: Track one wallet per spreadsheet with scheduled updates and metrics.

Master Dashboard: Aggregate multiple wallet spreadsheets into one dashboard (current value, P&L, last update).

Why do I see #REF! in Master Dashboard?

This usually means Google Sheets™ is waiting for IMPORTRANGE permission.

Open the Master Dashboard sheet and click Allow access in the affected cell.

How is my data handled?

Wallet addresses, schedule settings, and generated analytics are stored in your Google Sheets™ document and related Google Apps Script™ properties/cache.

To fetch DeFi data, wallet address and query parameters are sent to supported third-party DeFi APIs.

For full details, review our Privacy Policy.

Why is update time different from my local expectation?

Schedule execution depends on selected timezone and Google Apps Script™ trigger behavior.

If timing looks wrong, verify the timezone field in the sidebar and re-submit settings.

What should I include when contacting support?

Email defiassist@gmail.com and include:

  • the issue summary and steps to reproduce,
  • whether you are using Wallet Tracker or Master Dashboard,
  • screenshot of the sidebar or affected sheet,
  • exact error message text.
